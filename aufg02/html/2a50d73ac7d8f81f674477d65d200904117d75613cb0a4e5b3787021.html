Workshop Excel

Learn Excel with our professional IHK trainer. On the second day, a part of the workshop is devoted to VBA. Confirmation of participation will be issued if required. With intercultural brunch. Workshop in German.

Sat for beginners and Sun for advanced levels.

The event is eligible for the Study&Stay-Certificate from the h_da . You have to register for the program, to be able to get the certificate. Every student from the "Hochschule Darmstadt" can register. Please indicate if you are a part of the program with your registration e-mail.

Registration: team@itt.stwda.de

Time: 10am - 4.30 pm
