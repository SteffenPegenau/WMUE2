Drawing on his life experience, Tongan MC Rizván doesn't shy away from showing heart in the booth. Rapping over a beat that conjures up memories of golden era 90's tracks, Rizván takes us back to a time when hip-hop featured raw story-tellers.

64 Bars is a project formulated by David Dallas and Red Bull Studios Auckland, where handpicked MCs hit the booth and lay down ‘64 bars’. Straight raps - no hooks, no bells or whistles.
