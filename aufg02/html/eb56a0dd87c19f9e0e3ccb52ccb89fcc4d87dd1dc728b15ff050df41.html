On a record-breaking weekend for goals in the Bundesliga this season, there was a clear victor in the BLMVP vote for Matchday 33...

The winner

Arjen Robben (Bayern Munich)

  • Provided an assist and scored the match-winning goal in injury time as Bayern turned around a nine-goal thriller at second-placed Leipzig.

Time to vote for MD33's #BLMVP! Which of these four players gets your vote?@ArjenRobben - @andrejkramaric9 - @yoshimuto18 - #Lasogga

— Bundesliga English (@Bundesliga_EN) May 13, 2017

Other candidates

Yoshinori Muto (Mainz)

  • Came off the bench at half-time to turn around a 2-0 deficit for Mainz in the Rhine-Main derby against Eintracht Frankfurt.

Andrej Kramaric (Hoffenheim)

  • Two goals and an assist for the Croatian propelled Hoffenheim into a 5-0 lead at Werder Bremen and draw TSG level with Borussia Dortmund in third.

Pierre-Michel Lasogga (Hamburg)

  • Came off the bench with five minutes to go and scored the equaliser against Schalke to leave Hamburg's fate in their own hands on the final weekend.
